---
title: Certificate Templates
description: How to create and configure certificate templates with custom branding, signers, and layout options.
---

Certificate Templates define the appearance of your generated certificates — the title, subtitle, signers, logos, and layout.

You'll find them in the sidebar under **Printing > Certificate Templates**.

## Creating a Template

1. Click **Add Certificate Type**
2. Enter a **name** and **subtitle** for the certificate
3. Upload your **organization logo** and choose its position
4. Add up to two **signers** with name, title, and signature image
5. Customize the **award line** text (appears above the recipient's name)
6. Save

## Logo Positions

- **Top Left / Top Right** — small logo in the header area
- **Bottom Left / Bottom Right** — small logo in the footer area
- **Header Image** — large centered image spanning the top of the certificate

## Notes

- Templates are reusable across multiple certificate generations
- Signature images should be PNG files with transparent backgrounds for best results
- The certificate uses a landscape letter (11" x 8.5") layout
