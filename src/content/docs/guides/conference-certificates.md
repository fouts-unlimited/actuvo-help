---
title: Conference Certificates
description: How to generate attendance certificates, badges, rosters, and directories for conference attendees.
---

The printing suite generates professional materials for your conferences — attendance certificates, name badges, rosters, and attendee directories.

You'll find these in the sidebar under **Printing**.

## Attendance Certificates

1. Go to **Printing > Certificates**
2. Select the conference
3. Choose which attendees to include
4. Click **Generate** to create a multi-page PDF with one certificate per attendee

## Name Badges

1. Go to **Printing > Badges**
2. Select the conference
3. Upload your organization logo (optional)
4. Click **Generate** to create a printable badge sheet

## Attendance Roster

Go to **Printing > Attendance Roster** to generate a check-in sheet with attendee names and signature lines.

## Attendee Directory

Go to **Printing > Attendee Directory** to generate a directory with attendee photos and contact information.

## Notes

- All printing features filter attendees by conference registration status
- Certificates require a signature image on the conference configuration
- Badge layouts are designed for standard label sheets
