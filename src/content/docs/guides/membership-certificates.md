---
title: Membership Certificates
description: How to generate and print professional membership certificates for network members.
---

Membership Certificates generates PDF certificates for members of your network. Each certificate uses a template you configure with your organization's branding, signers, and layout.

You'll find it in the sidebar under **Printing > Membership Certificates**.

## Generating Certificates

1. Go to **Printing > Membership Certificates**
2. Select the **certificate template** to use
3. Choose which members to include (active, specific tier, or all)
4. Click **Generate** to create the PDF
5. Print or download the result

## Notes

- Certificate templates are configured under **Printing > Certificate Templates**
- Each template supports up to two signers with signature images
- Certificates include the member's name, tier, dates, and a unique certificate number
